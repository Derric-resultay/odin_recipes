Delicious Hotdogs

Description

Indulge in the savory delight of a classic hotdog, where simplicity meets satisfaction in every bite. Begin with plump, juicy sausages, carefully grilled to perfection until they boast a tantalizing charred exterior, locking in their succulent flavors. Nestled within a soft, toasted bun, these sizzling sausages await a cascade of toppings.

Generously drizzle tangy mustard and creamy mayonnaise, followed by a sprinkling of diced onions for a punch of freshness. Finally, crown this culinary masterpiece with a scattering of crisp, golden-brown onions and a dash of zesty relish, completing a symphony of flavors that dance harmoniously on the palate.

Ingredients

  • Â¼ cup butter
  • 1 Walla Walla or other sweet onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 (4 ounce) package cream cheese
  • 4 hot dogs, or your favorite sausages
  • 4 hot dog buns
  • brown mustard
  • 1 cup sauerkraut

Steps

  1. Preheat grill or grill pan for medium-high heat.

  2. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add onions, and cook slowly until the onions have softened and turned deep brown, about 15 minutes. Warm the cream cheese over low heat in a small skillet until very soft.

  3. Grill hot dogs until well browned. Lightly grill hot dog buns on both sides.

  4. To assemble cheese dogs, spread warm cream cheese on toasted hot dog bun, add hot dog or sausage, top with onions, mustard and sauerkraut, if desired.
